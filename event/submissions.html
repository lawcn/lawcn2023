All accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings, with ISBN (see proceedings of LAWCN 2017, LAWCN 2019, and LAWCN 2021). We will have the following journal options for publishing the best papers:

  • Full and short papers (English) as chapter book in Communication in Computer and Information Science - CCIS (ISSN 1865-0929) - (https://link.springer.com/conference/lawcn)
  • Full papers (English) as regular paper in the special issue "Interdisciplinary Approaches for Human Cognition: Expanding Perspectives on the Mind" in the INTERNATIONAL JOURNAL OF PSYCHOLOGICAL RESEARCH- IJPR (ISSN 2011-7922) - (https://revistas.usb.edu.co/index.php/IJPR/index)
  • Full and short papers (English, Spanish, Portuguese) as regular papers in the special issue "Engineering/computational methods applied to Psychology and neuroscience" in the Ingenierías USBMED journal (ISSN 2027-5846) - (https://revistas.usb.edu.co/index.php/IngUSBmed)
  • Abstract and short papers in Conference proceedings edited by Fondo Editorial Institución Universitaria de Envigado (IUE) - with ISBN.

Submitted papers will undergo a peer review process, coordinated by the International Program Committee, based on technical quality, originality, significance, clarity, and relevance to LAWCN 2023. Authors of accepted papers must present their work in the workshop. The full guide of authors and paper submission system is in the next link (with previous registration): https://revistas.iue.edu.co/index.php /lawcn/about/submissions
